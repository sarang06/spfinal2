Blended Learning is the revolution, Education has been waiting for

Posted By QED Technologies on October 03, 2018

The teaching landscape is changing very fast. The emergence of technologies and widespread integration of these technologies into our society, combined with access to the high speed Internet has integrally changed teaching-learning in just a few years. Rapidly changing landscapes should be an indicator to show that teaching-learning methods need to evolve to keep up with the change and incorporate integrated technologies into the teaching-learning model.

What is Blended Learning

Blended Learning is a mixture of learning methods that incorporate multiple teaching models. Most frequently technology based eLearning and traditional face-to-face teaching. Blended learning is a natural evolution of education because of the growing accessibility of eLearning, online resources and at the same time pressing need of a teacher in the learning space.

A blended learning approach ensures that the learner is engaged actively and has an element of personalized learning experience. Blended approach entirely changes the teaching –learning from passive class room delivery to active discussion and activity based engagement.

Blended learning is important because it breaks down the traditional model of delivery, one that don’t work for all students and now with access to technologies and resources we can tailor the learning experience for each student.

Conclusion

Blended learning offers flexible time frames that can be personalized to each person, offering them the ability to learn at their own pace at their own time When technology is integrated into school lessons, learners are more likely to be interested in, focused on, and excited about the subjects they are studying. It helps a lot improving understanding and increasing information retention. Blended learning offers a multitude of real-world skills, that directly translate into life skills.
